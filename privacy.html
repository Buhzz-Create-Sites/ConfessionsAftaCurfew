This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us including:

  • Email address when you join the Afta Curfew Tribe or submit a question
  • Phone number when you opt in to receive text message alerts
  • Name or alias when you submit a question or confession

  • To send you new episode alerts, merch drop notifications, and live show updates via email

Third Party Services

We use the following third party services to operate CAC. Each has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data:

  • Formspree — receives submissions from our Ask Us form and routes them to our team email.
  • YouTube — our episodes are hosted on YouTube. Watching embedded videos may be subject to YouTube's privacy policy.
