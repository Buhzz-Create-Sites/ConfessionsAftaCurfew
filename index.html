Who We Are

Afta Curfew

Confessions Afta Curfew is Philadelphia's most unfiltered adult podcast. We built this show around one simple idea. The truth gets louder after curfew. When the lights go low and the guards come down people finally say what they really mean. That is where we live.

Buhzz Taylor

Host & Co-Founder

Former nightlife manager, booking agent and operations director turned filmmaker and podcast host. Buhzz brings over a decade of real nightlife experience to every conversation. He has seen it all from the inside and he is not afraid to say it.

Luv Bug

Co-Host & Resident Dancer

Dancer, entertainer and the emotional core of Confessions Afta Curfew. Luv Bug brings the lived experience, the real stories and the kind of honesty that makes people stop scrolling. Every episode she tells all in her signature segment Luv Tells All.

CAC is not just a podcast. It is a content ecosystem built for real conversations, viral moments and a community of people who are tired of surface level talk. We call them the Afta Curfew Tribe. If you found us you already belong.

Your Turn

Confess Afta Curfew

Got a confession? Call our hotline and leave your voicemail. The best ones get played live on the show and we respond in real time. Keep it honest. Keep it grown.

(215) 703-8490

Available 24 Hours · Anonymous · No Judgment

You got something nobody knows? A secret you have been sitting on? Something that happened in the club, in a relationship, in the streets or behind closed doors that you have never told a single soul?

This is your line. Call it. Leave your voicemail confession. We will never reveal who you are. Your voice can be kept anonymous. Your secret stays yours unless you want it heard.

The best confessions get played live on Confessions Afta Curfew and we respond to them in real time. No names. No judgment. Just the truth afta curfew.

What Can You Confess?

  • ✦ You saw something in the club you were never supposed to see
  • ✦ You fell in love with someone in a nightlife space and never told them
  • ✦ A man or woman said something to you in the club that changed your life
  • ✦ You spent money you did not have chasing something that was never real
  • ✦ You held it down for someone and they never knew how much it cost you
  • ✦ You heard something in the club you were never supposed to hear
  • ✦ You caught feelings for someone whose lifestyle you could not handle
  • ✦ A dancer, bartender or bottle girl changed how you see the whole game
  • ✦ You stayed loyal to someone in the streets or behind bars and it broke you
  • ✦ You were in a situationship nobody around you knew about
  • ✦ Something happened after hours that you have never spoken about out loud
  • ✦ You used someone for what they had and you still think about it
  • ✦ You were the trick, the simp, the fraud or the one who got played and learned from it
